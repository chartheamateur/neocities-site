A List of my Favorite Websites

This is a list of some of my favorite websites that I recommend visiting!

Every site I list here is unique in some way. (Such as presentation, utility, content etc.)

Social media mega-platforms, shopping sites, forums, or sites of similar size are not listed here.

Alright, so in no particular order...

GifCities. A search engine for discovering animated GIFs from archived GeoCities sites.

PrivacyTools. A site dedicated to spreading awareness for easily accessible tools and services for improving online privacy.

Based Cooking. A simplistic, ad-free website full of user-submitted recipes.

Nitter. A Twitter mirror that allows users to view tweets without having to interact with other users or enable javascript.
